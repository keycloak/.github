# Community Code of Conduct

Keycloak follows the [CNCF Code of Conduct](https://github.com/cncf/foundation/blob/main/code-of-conduct.md).

## Reporting
If you suspect violations of the Code of Conduct in the Keycloak community, please contact the Keycloak Code of Conduct (CoC) Committee via `keycloak-coc@googlegroups.com`.

## Enforcement

Our primary goal is to foster a constructive and welcoming environment for all Keycloak contributors and users.
To protect this space, we actively enforce our Code of Conduct. If a community member's behavior falls short of these
guidelines, our CoC Commitee will intervene. Depending on the situation, this may range from a gentle reminder, up to
a temporary suspension or permanent ban for severe or repeated issues.
We take these steps carefully to ensure Keycloak remains a great place for everyone to collaborate.

If you have been banned from any Keycloak community spaces (such as GitHub, mailing lists, or Slack) and wish to appeal
the decision, please contact the Code of Conduct Committee at `keycloak-coc@googlegroups.com`. In your email, please include
your username, the platform where the ban occurred, and a brief, objective explanation of why you are appealing. Appeals
are reviewed by the Committee on a case-by-case basis.
